AI Journal Companion - Help & FAQ

Getting Started

  1. Open the app and type your journal entry in the text field.
  2. Tap Analyze to send it to the AI.
  3. The AI will return your dominant emotion and a short piece of advice.
  4. You can save multiple entries, sort them, and search them later.
  5. Use the Chart button to view your emotions in a pie chart.
  6. Drag and drop entries onto the trash icon to delete them.

FAQs

1. What does the app do?

The app analyzes your journal entries to detect emotions and provide short, supportive advice.

2. How does the emotion analysis work?

Your text is sent to a backend (FastAPI + Ollama model) which processes it and returns JSON with an emotion and advice.

3. What emotions can it detect?

It can detect common emotions such as JOY, SADNESS, ANGER, FEAR, DISGUST, SURPRISE, and NEUTRAL.

4. Why can’t I open links in the Help page?

This help guide is embedded and designed to stay inside the app. External links are disabled for simplicity.

5. How do I sort and search my entries?

You can choose sorting and searching methods from the dropdown menus. Sorting uses Bubble, Insertion, or Selection Sort. Searching uses Binary Tree, Hash Map, or Doubly Linked List.

6. Can I reset or clear all entries?

Yes. Use the drag-and-drop feature to delete entries one by one, or clear all from the options menu if available.
